School Dress Codes

Background

In 1969, the U.S. Supreme Court established the first school dress code law. According to Wikipedia, dress codes were initially created "to differentiate themselves from other people". Nowadays, schools have taken dress codes way too far by targeting girls because they think girls' clothing is distracting. My question is "Why are boys looking in the first place"? Also schools don't seem to be concerned if girls are looking at other girls. Why should a girl get in trouble for being "distracting" and having a boy look at her? It is not her fault.
